## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how VoxelFold ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

VoxelFold is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (or don't collect), how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## Information We Collect

### Data We DO Collect

**Game Progress Data (Stored Locally Only)**

- Current game state (tile positions and values)  
- High score  
- Game settings and preferences  

**Important:** All game data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, store, or access this data on any external servers.

### Data We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs  
- Analytics or usage statistics  
- Cookies or tracking data  
- Photos, contacts, or other personal data  
- Network information  
- Any data that leaves your device  

---

## How We Use Your Information

The game data stored locally on your device is used solely to:

- Save your game progress  
- Restore your game state when you return to the app  
- Remember your preferences and settings  

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard UserDefaults system. This data:

- Remains on your device  
- Is protected by iOS's built-in security features  
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app  
- Is backed up with your device backups (if enabled in iOS settings)  
- Is never transmitted over the internet  

### No Server Storage

We do not operate any servers or cloud storage for VoxelFold. Your game data is never uploaded to external servers.

VoxelFold does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

## Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union (and specifically Spain), you have the following rights:

### Right to Access

You can access all your game data at any time by viewing it within the app. Since data is stored locally, you have full access.

### Right to Deletion

You can delete your game data at any time by:

- Using the "New Game" button to start fresh  
- Uninstalling the app (deletes all local data)  
- Using iOS Settings to clear app data  

### Right to Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it through iOS's backup and restore functionality.

### Right to Object

You can object to data processing at any time by simply not using the app or uninstalling it.

### Right to Rectification

You can modify your game data at any time by playing the game and changing your progress.

### No Automated Decision-Making

VoxelFold does not use any automated decision-making or profiling.

- Clear the data using the app's "New Game" function  
- Uninstall the app

We do not retain any data because we do not collect or transmit any data to external servers.
